# Results, 2026-07-18 (scout run, single-pass)

**Machine:** Apple M5 Pro, arm64. Host has 15 cores / 48 GB RAM. the Docker Desktop VM exposes 7 cores / 7.2 GB to the container (the gap is the VM, not the machine). Hostname intentionally not recorded.
**Engines:** `llama-bench` (speed), `llama-completion` (accuracy), from the PrismML llama.cpp fork (`prism` branch), built CPU-only.
**Caveat (read first):** this is a **single-pass scout run**. Every number below is one sample, no variance reported, no statistical confidence. The multi-pass redesign in [docs/eval-redesign-plan.md](docs/eval-redesign-plan.md) exists precisely to fix this. Treat these as direction, not decision-grade.

### 8b-1bit
**Did not produce valid JSON in any case.** The 8B model emits a reasoning preamble ("Okay, the user wants me to reply with a JSON array...") into stdout and never emits the array itself. `extract_json_array()` correctly returns `None`. This is a prompting failure (8B "thinks out loud" before answering), not a harness bug, but it's the exact kind of failure the capture plan must record rather than collapse into "0%". Raw per-case latencies 29-57s.

## What the numbers say (single-pass, directional only)

1. **The quote architecture wins at 1.7B** (+100pts citation accuracy at every temp), because direct mode at 1.7B stops after one claim or misattributes, a small-model capability gap. When 1.7B *does* emit valid JSON in quote mode, `quote_match` is 100%, so the deterministic resolver works on this scale.

2. **Direct mode wins at 4B** (perfect 100/100/100 at every temp, faster). Quote mode *degrades* at temp>0 (75% cite_acc, 81% quote_hit).

3. **The research's "never let the model localize" conclusion was drawn at 8B-12B+ on exact-span F1, not marker matching.** Our metric (did it emit the right `[1]`) is strictly easier. So at marker-matching on small docs, 4B direct genuinely works, even though it would likely still fail FullCite's exact-span test. **The research and our measurement aren't contradicting. they measure different things.**

4. **8B needs a different prompting strategy**, not more passes. It "thinks" before answering. a chat-template or stop-string fix is required before it's even benchmarkable.

## What's NOT answered here (why you want the multi-pass redesign)

- No variance. A single sample per cell. The 1.7b-direct 14.5s at t=0 vs 2.7s at t=0.5 is suspect, one slow case dominates, but unmeasured.
- Only temp swept. top-k/top-p/penalties held at card defaults.
- The 8B failure is collapsed to "0%" with the root cause invisible in the summary, captures would have shown the preamble.
- 27B-tier and ternary tiers beyond 1.7b/4b not run in the accuracy matrix.
